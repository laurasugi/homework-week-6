Undeveloped Hot Springs

Nimrod hot springs

Nimrod is a natural, undeveloped warm spring outside of Missoula.

Features: The spring pool is quite wide and deep. There is also an underground rock cave that you can get to by swimming through an underwater tunnel.

Temperature: Around 70 degrees Fahrenheit. On the cooler side for a hot spring which makes it an excellent spring to visit in warmer months.
