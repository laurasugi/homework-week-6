Lolo hot springs

Lolo hot springs is a developed hot spring on the Montana-Idaho border.

Features: There are two outdoor and indoor pools, and the hot springs is connected to a lodge that has an additional indoor pool as well as private soaking tubs. Additional ammenities include a bar, restauraunt, bonfires and many nearby trails for hiking or horseback riding.

Temperature: The indoor pool is around 102 degrees farenheit and is open year round. The outdoor pool is occasionally closed during winter because it can get too cold.
